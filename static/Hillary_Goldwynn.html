Rogue the Doberman

told by Rogue

Intelligent & fearless junkyard dog.

As a Doberman, my fierce reputation precedes me. We're feared by those who don't know them, stereotyped as highly aggressive and vicious. True, we're a formidable guardian, but we're usually a gentle, watchful, and loving dog. We don't go looking for trouble, but we're fearless and will defend our family and turf if we perceive danger.

He enjoys being part of a family. They like to be close to those they love and, when this love is present, they're a natural protector. They're trustworthy with their family's children, friends, and guests as long as the pooch is treated kindly.
